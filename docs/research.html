Overview

My research interests include cryptography and its applications to secure biometrics, data-centric security, and network security. Since joining the French Air and Space Force Academy, I have also developed an interest in communication infrastructures in the aerospace domain, especially for service continuity, distributed computing in space environments, and security issues related to applications of defense interest.
